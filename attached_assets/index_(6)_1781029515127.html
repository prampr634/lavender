How It Works

A clear, collaborative process — so you always know what's happening and what comes next.

01

Discovery Call

We hop on a quick call or chat to talk through your goals, your audience, and what you need from your website. This shapes everything that follows.

→
02

Proposal & Quote

I put together a clear scope of work, timeline, and flat-rate quote. No ambiguity — you know exactly what you're getting before we start.

→
03

Design & Build

I get to work building your site — handling design, code, and content placement. You'll see a preview before anything is finalized.

→
04

Your Feedback

You review the site and share any changes. We go through your revision rounds until everything feels exactly right.

→
05

Launch

Your site goes live. I handle deployment, make sure everything works perfectly, and hand over everything you need to manage it going forward.
